A Story of Hope and Education

Who We Are

We are a grassroots non-profit fueled by a single belief: every child deserves the key to unlock their own future. That key is education.

Born in the heart of Delhi, Basti ki Pathshala is more than a school; it's a safe haven, a community hub, and a launchpad for dreams that were once thought impossible. We fight poverty and inequality with books, blackboards, and boundless compassion.

Our Journey

From a humble beginning to a beacon of hope.

2018
The First Class

With 5 students and a borrowed mat, our founder holds the first class under a Banyan tree in a Delhi slum, sparking a small light of hope.

2020
A Roof Overhead

Thanks to local support, we moved into a small, rented room, protecting our 50+ students from the harsh sun and rain for the first time.

2022
Beyond the Books

We launched our Mid-Day Meal program, ensuring that no child has to learn on an empty stomach. Enrollment crosses 200 students.

2025
Looking Ahead

With over 500 students and a team of dedicated volunteers, we aim to introduce digital literacy classes and expand to a neighboring community.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every action we take.

Compassion

We lead with empathy, understanding the unique challenges each child faces.

Integrity

We are transparent and accountable in our work to earn the trust of our community.

Empowerment

We provide tools and knowledge, not just aid, to create self-reliant individuals.
